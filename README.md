## Why This Tool?

You're in Japan and need help but don't speak Japanese. This tool gives you ready-to-use phrases for police, hospitals, and disasters — tap to copy to clipboard.

## Features

- **4 emergency categories** — Medical, Police, Disaster, and Daily situations
- **Tap to copy** — One tap copies the Japanese phrase to your clipboard
- **Romaji included** — Pronunciation guide for every phrase
- **Works offline** — Install as a PWA and use without internet
- **Mobile-first** — Designed for quick access on your phone

## How It Works

1. Pick a category (Medical, Police, Disaster, or Daily)
2. Find the phrase you need
3. Tap to copy — paste it into a message or show your screen

> **Try it now:** [https://humancronadmin.github.io/japan-emergency-phrases/](https://humancronadmin.github.io/japan-emergency-phrases/)

## Privacy

- All data stays **in your browser** — nothing is sent to any server
- Works offline after first visit (PWA)
