**Invisible Solo Builder Update: Japan Emergency Phrases, a life-saving tool for foreigners in crisis, is now available.

Hello indie hackers!

I would like to introduce my latest project, [Japan Emergency Phrases](https://humancronadmin.github.io/japan-emergency-phrases/). It is a one-tap tool to copy Japanese emergency phrases for foreigners in a crisis situation. As a solo builder, I am all about transparency, so I will delve into the motivation, functionality, and future plans for this project.

**Posing the Problem

As a foreigner traveling to Japan, emergency communication is an important issue. Whether it is a medical emergency, a lost child, or a natural disaster, it is important to be able to communicate your needs quickly and accurately. However, language barriers can be a major obstacle, leading to delays in response or worsening the situation.

**Solutions

Japan Emergency Phrases aims to bridge this gap by providing a simple, easy-to-use tool for foreigners to copy and paste emergency phrases in Japanese. With one tap, you can access phrases such as "I need help," "Where is the hospital?" and "I am lost." These phrases can be copied and shared with authorities, medical personnel, or other individuals who can offer assistance.

**Original Value Proposition.

Simplicity and speed are the hallmarks of Japan Emergency Phrases. Unlike other language learning apps or phrasebooks, this tool is designed specifically for time-sensitive emergencies. The one-tap copy feature allows users to quickly access the phrase they need without wasting time navigating complex menus or typing in phrases.

**Features

* Copy emergency phrases with a single tap.
* Phrases categorized by situation (medical, lost, natural disaster, etc.)
* Simple and intuitive interface
* No ads, no tracking, completely free
