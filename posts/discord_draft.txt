Hey devs, just wanted to give a shoutout to a really useful tool I stumbled upon: https://humancronadmin.github.io/japan-emergency-phrases/. If you've ever traveled to Japan or know someone who has, you know how stressful it can be to navigate an emergency situation when you don't speak the language. 

This tool provides one-tap copy for essential emergency phrases in Japanese, which can be a total lifesaver in a crisis. No more scrambling to find the right words or trying to pronounce unfamiliar characters. Just tap, copy, and paste - it's a huge relief to have this kind of support when you need it most.

I actually covered this tool (along with 12 others) in a roundup article on DEV.to, but I wanted to give it a special mention here because of its unique value prop. If you have friends or family traveling to Japan, be sure to pass this along - it's a simple but powerful resource that can make a big difference in an emergency.
